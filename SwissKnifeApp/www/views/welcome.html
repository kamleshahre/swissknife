Welcome to Swiss Knife App!

SwissKnifeApp is the solution for all your festival needs. Scroll down to see what features are available.

Offline Functionality

Some of the functionality of the app will work if you are offline. This includes:

Festival Lineup

See who and when is playing.

Festival Map

Not sure where you are? Take a look at the map.

Online Functionality

You'll need to be connected to the internet with your device in order to use the following content:

Friends

What are your friends doing right now?

Photos

Take pictures and upload them to your photo stream.

Tickets

Scan your tickets and have a proof of purchase with you at all times.

Twitterwall

See what people are tweeting about this festival.

If you are not connected to the internet, these features will not work.
